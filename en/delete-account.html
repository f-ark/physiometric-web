Account Deletion Request

Last updated: June 17, 2026

⚠️ Important: The account deletion process is irreversible. When you delete your account, your progress, exercise history, and all data related to the app will be permanently removed.

Method 1: Quick Deletion In-App (Recommended)

If you have access to the app, you can delete your account directly from your device most quickly:

  • Open the Physio Metric app.
  • Go to the Profile tab.
  • Tap the "Delete Account" button and confirm.

Method 2: Account Deletion Request via Email

If you have uninstalled the app or don't have access, you can request account deletion via email:

  • Contact us from the email address you registered or logged in with.
  • Send the email to ferdiarikan@gmail.com.
  • Write "Account Deletion Request - Physio Metric" in the subject line.
  • Once your request is verified, your data will be permanently deleted within 48 hours.

What Happens When Your Account is Deleted?

Data Type Deletion Status
Session Information
(Firebase Auth)		 Permanently deleted as soon as the request is processed.
Analysis and Health Data
(Posture, Exercises, etc.)		 Not stored on our servers. Completely disappears the moment you uninstall the app from your device.
Error Logs
(Crashlytics)		 Technical crash records and technical identifiers linked to the app installation may be processed for debugging purposes and are automatically deleted from the system within a maximum of 90 days.
