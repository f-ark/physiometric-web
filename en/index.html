How It Works

Start in 4 steps
from either path

With an expert prescription or your own analysis — the same powerful loop.

📸
1

Digital Posture Analysis

Take a posture photo with your camera. The machine learning model automatically detects 33 joints and analyzes your posture based on 22 rules.

📍
2

Pain Map

Indicate pain points and their severity on your body. Analysis + pain data combine to create your optimal exercise plan.

💪
3

3-Day Exercise Cycle

Do your exercises with 3D animated voice coaching. Unfinished exercises don't disappear, they carry over to the next day.

📊
4

Proof of Progress

Re-analyze at the end of the cycle. See the change in your score and prove your concrete progress.

📋
1

Create an Exercise Program

📱
2

Share via QR Code

Your prescription automatically turns into an encrypted QR code. Your patient accesses the program instantly by scanning the QR.

👁️
3

Patient Tracking

View your patient's exercise completion rate from the dashboard. Monitor compliance and update the program when necessary.

⭐
4

Level Up Your Expertise

Your level increases with your sharing count and patient compliance rates. Grow your digital portfolio.
